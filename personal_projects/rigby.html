Motivation

During my development of the system I call RABBIT, I quickly hit a fundamental design split. The need for both high-resolution visual data and real-time depth sensing led to the creation of RIGBY as the visual component of the broader sensing architecture.

RIGBY serves as the high-fidelity visual sensor in the network, providing detailed imagery that complements the depth and motion data from other sensors in the system. This distributed approach allows for more sophisticated computer vision applications while maintaining real-time performance.
