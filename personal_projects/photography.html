Photography

One of the first engineering/art crossovers I came across. I got my first camera when I was 10 years old, and immediately fell in love. The feeling of millions of photons colliding and energizing buckets on a microscopically tiny city of diodes that spits out a cloud of colors that organizes to freeze a moment or tell a story... well, it fed both sides of my brain in one meal.
