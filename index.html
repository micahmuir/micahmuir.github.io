Micah Muir Thomas

Hello.

My name is Micah. I'm a mechanical engineer, artist, and tinkerer currently living in Portland, Oregon.

I was born in South Africa, and while growing up lived in Singapore, Scotland, and California before settling back in PDX area.

Moving around all the time as a kid shaped my desire to bridge techniques and disciplines when it comes to the act of creation. In college, I couldn't decide whether to pursue fine art or get an engineering degree, so I did both. Professionally, I design and integrate hardware for robotic systems. Outside of work, I learn new skills by building different projects that span photography, live visuals, spatial sensing, imaging, and more. There's beauty in the overlap of science, art, and human emotion. Data doesn't have to live and die in a table. My goal is to leverage engineering to make better art, and vice versa.

These pages are intended to serve as an expansion of my professional resume, as well as a platform to share the things I work on in my spare time.

Thanks for stopping by ~
