Hello, I'm Micah. I'm an engineer, artist, and tinkerer currently based in Portland, Oregon.

I'm geared by the overlap between science and design, between mechanics and aesthetics, and infinite potential of creative people in small groups. Both my parents were school teachers, and through their international teaching posts I gained an early and deep appreciation for lifelong learning, and the joy of using a broad skillset to tackle unique problems.

In college, I couldn't decide whether to pursue fine art or get an engineering degree, so I did both. Professionally, I design and integrate hardware for advanced robotic systems, but have worked across scales from tiny embedded smart glasses to building-size industrial systems. In and outside of work, I believe all things are connected whether we like it or not, and there's beauty in the overlap of science, art, and human emotion. Data doesn't have to live and die in a table. Broadly, my goal is to make the world safer, more beautiful, and to never stop learning.

These pages are intended to serve as an expansion of my professional resume, as well as a platform to share some of the relevant projects I work on in my spare time.

Thanks for stopping by ~
