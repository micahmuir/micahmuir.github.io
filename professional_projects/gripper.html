Vacuum Suction Gripper

*Parts of this project are projected by NDA, but I can speak about it in general terms.

I designed, tested, and deployed a 12-cup, 4 zone vacuum gripper to handle a variety of box sizes and weights in an automated warehouse. The gripper is almost entire 3d printed on a BambuLab X1C, and enabled us to quickly iterate on design changes as we learned what did and didn't worked in a real-world environment. The gripper can pick and place boxes from 4"x4"x4" up to 24"x24"x24", and weights up to 50 lbs. Each of the four zones can be controlled independently to enable precise handling of oddly shaped boxes. 4 Keyence laser sensors continuously monitor the distance to the box, and inform the controller when a good box seal has been created before executing the pick. Actual zone control is handled by a secondary base station with a valve bank, to minimize base payload weight.
