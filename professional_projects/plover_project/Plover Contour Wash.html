Plover Contour Wash

Plover Contour Wash

The Plover Contour wash was the first time I really got to work on something novel. It was a touchless car wash that would create a 3D point cloud of your car by scanning it with two lidar pucks. A toolpath was created that maintained the optimal cleaning distance from your car, and the toolhead would trace every contour of your vehicle.

I joined the startup as a junior design engineer, and my projects included the frame scene in the video, the X and Y linear axis and the acid-proof LED screens scene on the gantry carriage and the toolhead.

This was a foundational project in my career, where I gained exposure to the design of large structures, linux computing, real-world robotic design, and what it means to keep an industrial system running 24/7.
