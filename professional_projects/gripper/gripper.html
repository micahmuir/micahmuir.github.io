Vacuum Suction Gripper

*Parts of this project are protected by NDA, but I can speak about it in general terms.

I designed, tested, and deployed a 12-cup, 4 zone vacuum gripper to handle a variety of box sizes and weights in an automated warehouse. The gripper is almost entirely 3D printed on a BambuLab X1C, and enabled us to quickly iterate on design changes as we learned what did and didn't work in a real-world environment.

The gripper can pick and place boxes from 4"×4"×4" up to 24"×24"×24", and weights up to 50 lbs. Each of the four zones can be controlled independently to enable precise handling of oddly shaped boxes. 4 Keyence laser sensors continuously monitor the distance to the box, and inform the controller when a good box seal has been created before executing the pick.

Actual zone control is handled by a secondary base station with a valve bank, to minimize base payload weight.
