Palletizing Robot

I designed the metal superstructure for quad-parallel palletizing solution, and acted as primary mechanical engineer for the project from installation onwards.

I designed, simulated, and integrated the steel superstructure for this robot so that the company could begin populating the volume with off-the-shelf components. The structure had to be rigid enough to allow the upper dual XYZT gantry to accelerate fast enough to palletize four pallets simultaneously. This was successful, and the system was able to palletize at a rate of up to 900 CPH.

This was a R&D project, and I worked closely with it from conception through numerous upgrades and improvements as we utilized it in real-world inbound product scenarios. I saw this system through several key system upgrades such as:

  • 3D machine vision camera EOAT integration
  • Numerous crash protection safety systems
  • Axis modifications and upgrades
  • Safety compliance systems
