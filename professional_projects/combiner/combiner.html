Palletizing Robot

Mechanical design and hardware integration for quad-parallel palletizing solution

I acted as mechanical engineer, then eventually hardware lead on a unique palletizing robot. I designed the metal superstructure for quad-parallel palletizing solution. I worked closely with this machine from concept through to final installation, and provided many additional hardware improvements as we learned more about how this novel system operated in the real world.

I designed, simulated, and integrated the steel superstructure for this robot so that the company could begin populating the volume with off-the-shelf components. The structure had to be strong enough to allow the upper dual XYZT gantry to accelerate fast enough to palletize four pallets simultaneously.

This was a R&D project, and I worked closely with it from conception through numerous upgrades and improvements as we utilized it in real-world inbound product scenarios. I worked with this machine through concept and commissioning, as well as through several key system upgrades such as:

  • 3D machine vision camera EOAT integration
  • Numerous crash protection safety systems
  • Axis modifications and upgrades
  • Safety compliance systems
