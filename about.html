Micah Muir Thomas

Hi there, and welcome to my page! My name is Micah. I'm an engineer, artist, and tinkerer currently based in Portland, Oregon.

I was born in South Africa, and lived in Singapore, Scotland, and California before settling back in PDX area.

Moving around all the time as a kid definitely shaped my desire to bridge techniques and disciplines when it comes to the maker arts. In college, I couldn't decide whether to pursue fine art or get an engineering degree, so I did both. Professionally, I design and integrate hardware for robotic systems. In and outside of work, I believe all things are connected whether we like it or not, and there's beauty in the overlap of science, art, and human emotion. Data doesn't have to live and die in a table. My goal is to leverage engineering to make better art, and vice versa.

These pages are intended to serve as an expansion of my professional resume, as well as a platform to share the things I work on in my spare time.

Thanks for stopping by ~
